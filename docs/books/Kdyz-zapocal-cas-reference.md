## Key Arguments

1. Time was not invented by humans but given by the [[Anunnaki]]
2. The Sumerian calendar encoded the orbit of [[Nibiru]]
3. The [[Precese|precessional cycle]] of 25,920 years was known to the ancients
4. [[Posvátná-čísla|Sacred numbers]] (12, 60, 360, 3,600) encode astronomical knowledge
5. [[Stonehenge]] and other megalithic sites were astronomical observatories
