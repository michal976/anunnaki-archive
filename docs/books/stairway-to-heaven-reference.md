# Schody k nebesům — Reference Notes

Reference summary for **The Stairway to Heaven** (1980), the second book of Zecharia Sitchin's Earth Chronicles series.

## Bibliographic Information

- **Title:** *The Stairway to Heaven*
- **Author:** Zecharia Sitchin
- **Publisher:** St. Martin's Press
- **Publication Date:** 1980
- **Pages:** 352 (approx.)
- **Series:** Earth Chronicles, Book 2

## Chapter Summary

## Key Arguments

1. The Sinai Peninsula was the main spaceport
2. The [Temple Mount](../places/jerusalem.md) was the "Mount of the Crossing"
3. [Baalbek](../places/Baalbek.md) was the launch pad
4. [Great Pyramid](../places/giza.md) was a navigation beacon
5. [Gilgamesh](../characters/gilgamesh.md) sought immortality at the spaceport

## Key Quotes

> *"The Stairway to Heaven was not a ladder of dreams — it was a literal launch path to the stars."*

> *"Baalbek was not built by the Romans. The megalithic platform is the remnant of an Anunnaki spaceport."*
