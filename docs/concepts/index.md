## How Concepts Interrelate

These concepts form a coherent narrative:

1. **Arrival**: The Anunnaki landed at [Eridu](Eridu.md) and established [E.Din](E.DIN.md)
2. **Infrastructure**: They built [DUR.AN.KI](DUR.AN.KI.md) stations and a [spaceport](sinai-spaceport.md)
3. **Labor**: The [Igigi](Igigi.md) revolted, leading to the creation of [Adamu](Adamu.md)
4. **Knowledge**: The [ME](ME.md) and [Tablet Of Destinies](tablet-of-destinies.md) governed civilization
5. **Catastrophe**: The [Flood](../timeline/flood.md) and [nuclear destruction](../quotes/nuclear-destruction.md) reshaped history
6. **Technology**: [SHEM](SHEM.md) rockets and the [Šar](shar.md)/[NÉRU](neru.md) time cycles
7. **Conflicts**: The [Pyramid Wars](../timeline/pyramid-wars.md) and [migrations of the gods](migration-of-gods.md)
