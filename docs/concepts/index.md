# Concepts

This folder catalogs the core concepts, terms, and ideas that form the conceptual framework of Zecharia Sitchin's Earth Chronicles. These are the building blocks of the Anunnaki narrative.

## How Concepts Interrelate

These concepts form a coherent narrative:

1. **Arrival**: The [[Anunnaki]] landed at [[Eridu]] and established [[E.DIN]]
2. **Infrastructure**: They built [[DUR.AN.KI]] stations and a [[Sinaj-kosmodrom|spaceport]]
3. **Labor**: The [[Igigi]] revolted, leading to the creation of [[Adamu]]
4. **Knowledge**: The [[ME]] and [[Tabulka-osudů]] governed civilization
5. **Catastrophe**: The [[Potopa|Flood]] and [[Nukleární-zničení|nuclear destruction]] reshaped history
6. **Technology**: [[SHEM]] rockets and the [[Šar]]/[[NÉRU]] time cycles
7. **Conflicts**: The [[Pyramidové-války]] and [[Stěhování-bohů|migrations of the gods]]
