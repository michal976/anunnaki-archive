# Pyramid Wars

The **Pyramid Wars** were the series of conflicts among the Anunnaki that form the central narrative of Zecharia Sitchin's *The Wars of Gods and Men* (1985). These wars were fought over control of Earth's spaceport facilities and the supremacy among the gods.

## The Causes

The Pyramid Wars had multiple causes:

1. **Marduk's Ambition** — [Marduk](../characters/Marduk.md), firstborn son of [Enki](../characters/Enki.md), sought supremacy over the other Anunnaki and control of the spaceport
2. **Enlil vs. Enki** — The ancient rivalry between the two brothers intensified as Marduk challenged Enlil's authority
3. **The Spaceport** — Control of the spaceport at [Sippar](../places/Sippar.md) and the navigation beacons at [Giza](../places/giza.md) and [Baalbek](../places/Baalbek.md)
4. **Human Kingdoms** — The Anunnaki factions backed different human rulers and city-states
5. **Territorial Disputes** — The division of Earth's lands among the gods

## The Course of the Wars

### Phase 1: Marduk's Rise

Marduk established his power base at [Babylon](../places/Babylon.md) and sought to elevate it above [Nippur](../places/Nippur.md), Enlil's command center. He was aided by his father Enki and opposed by [Ninurta](../characters/Ninurta.md) (the warrior son of Enlil).

### Phase 2: The Confrontation

The conflict escalated:
- Marduk seized control of [Sippar](../places/Sippar.md), the spaceport
- [Enlil](../characters/Enlil.md) demanded the return of the spaceport
- [Ninurta](../characters/Ninurta.md) led military campaigns against Marduk's forces
- The "Tower of Babel" incident — Marduk's attempt to build a rocket launch tower

### Phase 3: The Nuclear Strike

Enlil authorized a nuclear strike against Sippar and [Ur](../places/Ur.md) to prevent Marduk from seizing the spaceport permanently. The weapon was delivered by [Ninurta](../characters/Ninurta.md) and possibly [Nergal](../characters/Nergal.md).

### Phase 4: The Aftermath

- The spaceport was destroyed
- Marduk fled into exile (to Egypt and the Americas)
- Enlil's authority was damaged
- The Anunnaki gradually withdrew from Earth

## The "Pyramid" Connection

The wars are called "Pyramid Wars" because Sitchin argued that the pyramids at Giza were part of the spaceport infrastructure. The Great Pyramid served as a navigation beacon, and control over it was strategically vital.
