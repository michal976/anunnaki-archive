# Michael Heiser — Theological Critique

Dr. [[Michael Heiser]] (1963–2023) was a biblical scholar and the most influential theological critic of Zecharia Sitchin's work. He held a PhD in Hebrew Bible and Semitic Languages and was a specialist in the divine council worldview of the Old Testament.

## Heiser's Key Criticisms

1. **The Divine Council** — Heiser argued that the biblical "sons of God" (*bene elohim*) were a divine council of spiritual beings created by Yahweh, not extraterrestrial Anunnaki
2. **The Nephilim** — Heiser argued that the Nephilim were the offspring of fallen angels (not Anunnaki) and human women
3. **Elohim** — The Hebrew word *elohim* means "spiritual being" or "god," not "extraterrestrial astronaut"
4. **Translation** — Heiser accused Sitchin of mistranslating key Hebrew and Sumerian terms
5. **Unseen Realm** — Heiser's book *The Unseen Realm* (2015) presented an alternative supernatural worldview
