# W. G. Lambert — Assyriological Critique

W. G. Lambert (1926–2011) was one of the foremost Assyriologists of the 20th century. He published critical editions of the [Enuma Elish](../sources/enuma-elish.md), [Atra Hasis](../sources/Atra-Hasis.md), and many other Mesopotamian texts.

## Lambert's Criticisms

1. Sitchin's astronomical interpretation of the Enuma Elish has no textual basis
2. The epic is a theological and political document, not an astronomical one
3. Sitchin's translations of key passages are inaccurate
4. The Sumerian King List is a literary construction, not historical record
