# Mainstream asyriologie (Mainstream Assyriology)

Mainstream Assyriology — the academic study of ancient Mesopotamia — has overwhelmingly rejected Zecharia Sitchin's work.

## Key Criticisms

1. **Translation Errors** — Sitchin's translations of Sumerian and Akkadian texts depart significantly from accepted scholarly readings
2. **Lack of Credentials** — Sitchin was not a trained Assyriologist
3. **Selective Citation** — Sitchin only used texts that supported his theory
4. **Misreading of Sources** — Sitchin misinterpreted the nature of Sumerian myths
5. **No Peer Review** — Sitchin's work was never published in academic journals
