# Nibiru kritika (Nibiru Criticism)

The existence of [[Nibiru]] as described by Zecharia Sitchin is not supported by mainstream astronomy.

## Astronomical Objections

1. **Orbital Mechanics** — A planet with a 3,600-year orbit as described would be detectable
2. **Gravitational Effects** — Such a planet would cause observable perturbations in the orbits of outer planets
3. **Infrared Signatures** — A large planet would be visible in infrared surveys
4. **No Evidence** — Decades of astronomical surveys have found no evidence of such a planet

## Planet Nine

The scientific search for "Planet Nine" (Batygin & Brown, 2016) is sometimes confused with Sitchin's Nibiru, but they are different concepts:
- Planet Nine: Neptune-sized, 10,000–20,000-year orbit
- Nibiru (Sitchin): Earth-sized, 3,600-year orbit
