# Pseudohistorie (Pseudohistory)

Mainstream scholars classify Zecharia Sitchin's work as **pseudohistory** — a body of work that claims to be historical but uses methods that are not accepted by the academic mainstream.

## Characteristics of Pseudohistory

1. **Selective Evidence** — Only evidence that supports the theory is cited
2. **Lack of Peer Review** — The work was not vetted by academic peers
3. **No Falsifiability** — The theory cannot be tested or disproven
4. **Conspiracy Narratives** — Claims that mainstream scholars are hiding the truth
5. **Misuse of Sources** — Ancient texts are misread and misinterpreted
