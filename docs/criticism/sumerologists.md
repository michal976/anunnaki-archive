# Sumerologists

The academic response to Zecharia Sitchin's work from professional Sumerologists.

## Leading Scholars

Key figures in Sumerology who have addressed or been relevant to the Sitchin debate:

- Samuel Noah Kramer — The pioneering Sumerologist whose translations Sitchin used
- Thorkild Jacobsen — Expert on Sumerian religion
- William W. Hallo — Expert on Sumerian literature
- Jeremy Black — Editor of the Sumerian lexicon
- W. G. Lambert — Editor of the Atra-Hasis epic

## The Academic Consensus

No accredited Sumerologist supports Sitchin's interpretations. The consensus is that Sitchin's work represents a misunderstanding of Sumerian language and culture.
