## 2. The Enuma Elish — Marduk Enters the Solar System

The Enuma Elish (literally "When on High") is the Babylonian creation epic, found in the library of Ashurbanipal. It is inscribed on seven clay tablets. Mainstream scholars call it mythology. Sitchin called it **astronomy**.

The epic describes the birth of the solar system:

> *"When on high the heavens had not been named,*
> *Below, the earth had not been called by name —*
> *Apsu, the Primordial, their begetter,*
> *Mummu and Tiamat — she who gave birth to them all —*
> *Had their waters mingled together."*

Sitchin read this as a description of the early solar system: **Apsu** (the Sun), **Tiamat** (a watery planet between Mars and Jupiter), and a host of smaller bodies.

Then a new celestial body appears:

> *"In the midst of Tiamat they made a star —*
> *The star Nibiru, the Planet of the Crossing,*
> *They decreed his destiny, gave him the bow,*
> *The bow as a weapon, and made him Marduk.*
> *Great in battle, the star that crosses the sky —*
> *Nibiru, who like a spear pierces Tiamat's heart!"*

**The astronomical reading (Sitchin):**

1. **Marduk = Nibiru** — a large, dark planet captured by the Sun's gravity
2. **Tiamat** — a watery planet that existed between Mars and Jupiter
3. **The collision** — Nibiru's inclined orbit brought it directly through Tiamat's path
4. **The spear** — one of Nibiru's satellites (the "bow" or "spear") struck Tiamat
5. **The result** — Tiamat was shattered: half became Earth, the other half became the **asteroid belt** and the **Kuiper Belt**

Tablet VII of the Enuma Elish explicitly identifies Nibiru and its path:

> *"Nibiru, who like a spear pierces Tiamat's heart —*
> *He who crosses the heavens and scans the worlds —*
> *When Nibiru rises, he fills the heart of the heavens!*
> *The stars of the sky he takes possession of like sheep —*
> *He who knows the path of the gods, the stars of the sky!"*

## 3. Nibiru's Orbit — The 3,600-Year Journey

The most startling claim in Sitchin's reading of Sumerian astronomy is Nibiru's orbit. Unlike the other planets, which travel in nearly circular orbits close to the ecliptic plane, Nibiru's path is described as:

- **Highly elliptical** — stretching from deep within the solar system (between Mars and Jupiter at perigee) far into the outer darkness of the Kuiper Belt and beyond (apogee)
- **Inclined** — crossing the orbital plane of the other planets at an angle
- **Retrograde** — moving in the opposite direction of the other planets
- **Period: 3,600 years** — one complete revolution

| Orbital Property | Sumerian Description | Modern Planet Nine Hypothesis |
|:----------------:|:--------------------:|:-----------------------------:|
| Orbit shape | Highly elliptical — "crossing the heart" | Highly elliptical (eccentricity 0.2–0.6) |
| Period | 3,600 years (1 SAR) | 10,000–20,000 years |
| Inclination | Crosses ecliptic | ~30° off ecliptic |
| Perigee | Between Mars and Jupiter | 200–300 AU from Sun |
| Direction | Retrograde ("crossing against") | Uncertain / retrograde possible |
| Moons | Multiple — "the bow" and "the spear" | Expected — captured KBOs |

**The SAR Unit:**

In Sumerian mathematics, the SAR was a numerical unit equal to 3,600. It was used to measure:

- 1 SAR = 3,600 years (celestial)
- 1 SAR = 3,600 shekels (weight)
- 1 SAR = 3,600 kur (volume)
- 1 SAR = 3,600 (pure number)

But the SAR was *also* used to measure the lifespans of the Anunnaki gods. The Sumerian King List records kings who ruled for absurdly long periods:

> *"After the kingship descended from heaven,*
> *the kingship was in Eridug.*
> *In Eridug, Alulim became king; he ruled for 28,800 years.*
> *Alaljar ruled for 36,000 years."*

These are **SAR multiples** — 8 SAR (28,800 years) and 10 SAR (36,000 years). Sitchin argued that the Sumerian King List was not recording human kings. It was recording **Anunnaki rulers** — beings from Nibiru, for whom one year on their home planet was equal to 3,600 Earth years.

**The implication:** Anunnaki who lived hundreds of thousands of Earth years were simply experiencing normal lifespans by Nibiru's standards — 50 to 100 Nibiru-years.

| Figure | Earth Years | Nibiru Years (SAR) |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|

| Alaljar | 36,000 | 10 |
| Enki (in legends) | ~144,000 | ~40 |

## 4. The Collision — Tiamat → Earth + Asteroid Belt

The Enuma Elish tells the story of a cosmic war. But Sitchin argued it was **a cosmic collision** — and the evidence is written in plain sight across the solar system.

**Tiamat** (from the Sumerian *TI.AMA.TUM* — "The Maiden of Life") was a large watery planet that orbited between Mars and Jupiter. When Nibiru first entered the solar system, its orbit brought it directly through Tiamat's path.

One of Nibiru's moons — the "bow" or "spear" — struck Tiamat, shattering it:

> *"The Lord raised the bow, his weapon, and said:*
> *'Now I will cut Tiamat in two — I will split her like a shellfish!'*
> *He struck her with the club — he split her in two.*
> *One half he set up as a covering for the heavens —*
> *He fixed a bolt, he stationed a guard.*
> *The other half — he trampled the earth beneath her."*

**Sitchin's reading:**

1. **The upper half** of Tiamat was pulverized into the **asteroid belt** (between Mars and Jupiter)
2. **The lower half** was displaced into a new orbit closer to the Sun — becoming **Earth**
3. **Tiamat's waters** became Earth's oceans — "the waters above and the waters below"
4. The **Kuiper Belt** and **scattered disc objects** are the debris field of the collision

**The scientific corroboration:**

- The asteroid belt contains a mass gap — there should be a full planet there, but there isn't
- The Earth has an anomalously large moon — consistent with a catastrophic collision (the Giant Impact Hypothesis)
- Earth's water content is anomalously high for an inner solar system planet
- The Earth's crust is geologically younger than the rest of the solar system
- The asteroid belt composition matches what you'd expect from a shattered watery planet

| Feature | Solar System Anomaly | Tiamat Collision Explanation |
|---------|---------------------|------------------------------|
| Asteroid belt mass | Only 4% of what a full planet should have | Tiamat was shattered |
| Earth's water | Too much for inner solar system | Inherited from Tiamat |
| Earth's Moon | Too large for Earth's size | Formed from collision debris |

### The Caltech Prediction (Batygin & Brown, 2016–Present)

In January 2016, Konstantin Batygin and Mike Brown — both at Caltech — published a paper titled *"Evidence for a Distant Giant Planet in the Solar System"* in *The Astronomical Journal*.

Their evidence:

1. **Six Kuiper Belt Objects** have orbits that cluster together in a statistically impossible way — probability less than 0.0001% by chance
2. The clustering can only be explained by a **massive unseen planet** herding them into alignment
3. The predicted planet has:
   - Mass: 5–10 Earth masses
   - Orbit: 200–800 AU from the Sun
   - Period: 10,000–20,000 years (Sitchin said 3,600 — but Nibiru's orbit may have changed after capture)
   - Eccentricity: 0.2–0.6 (elongated)
   - Inclination: ~30° off the ecliptic

> *"There are now five different lines of observational evidence pointing to the existence of Planet Nine. If you were to remove this explanation and imagine another object that is responsible for all of these phenomena, it would have to be an incredible coincidence."*
> — Konstantin Batygin (2024 interview)

### The IRAS Sighting (1983)

The **Infrared Astronomical Satellite (IRAS)** scanned the sky in 1983 and detected a mysterious object described in press reports at the time:

> *"A heavenly body possibly as large as the giant planet Jupiter and possibly so close to Earth that it would be part of this solar system has been found in the direction of the constellation Orion by an orbiting telescope..."*
> — *The Washington Post*, December 30, 1983

IRAS project scientist Gerry Neugebauer confirmed: *"It could be as large as Jupiter and is so close to Earth that it would be part of the solar system."*

Within weeks, the story was retracted and the data reclassified. NASA denied there was anything unusual.
