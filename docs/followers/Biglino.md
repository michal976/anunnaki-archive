# Mauro Biglino

**Mauro Biglino** (born 1950) is an Italian author who specializes in a "literal translation" of the Hebrew Bible, arguing that the Elohim were extraterrestrial beings.

## Key Works

- *The Book That Will Change Your Life* (2015)
- *The Bible Is Not a Sacred Book* (2020)
- Various YouTube documentaries

## Key Claims

- The Hebrew *Elohim* means "powerful ones" — not God
- Yahweh was an Anunnaki leader, not the supreme deity
- The Ark of the Covenant was a communication device
- Angels were Anunnaki astronauts

## Relationship to Sitchin

Biglino extends Sitchin's work with his own linguistic analysis of the Hebrew text, providing additional support for the Anunnaki interpretation of the Bible.
