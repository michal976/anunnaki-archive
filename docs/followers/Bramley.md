# William Bramley

**William Bramley** is the author of *The Gods of Eden* (1989), which argues that humanity has been under the control of a hidden power structure since ancient times.

## Key Claims

- The Anunnaki created a "Think Tank" to control humanity
- Secret societies have maintained Anunnaki control
- Wars have been engineered to control population
- Religious institutions are vehicles of control
