# David Hatcher Childress

**David Hatcher Childress** is an American author and explorer known for his research on lost civilizations and ancient technology.

## Key Works

- *Lost Cities of Atlantis, Ancient Europe & the Mediterranean*
- *Vimana Aircraft of Ancient India & Atlantis*
- *Technology of the Gods*
- Lost Cities series

## Relationship to Sitchin

Childress frequently cites Sitchin's work on the Anunnaki and explores the technological aspects of the ancient astronaut hypothesis.
