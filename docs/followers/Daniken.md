# Erich von Däniken

**Erich von Däniken** (born 1935) is a Swiss author best known for his 1968 book *Chariots of the Gods?*, which launched the modern ancient astronaut hypothesis.

## Key Works

- *Chariots of the Gods?* (1968)
- *Gods from Outer Space* (1970)
- *The Gold of the Gods* (1972)
- *Eyes of the Sphinx* (1996)

## Relationship to Sitchin

Däniken preceded Sitchin and laid the groundwork for the ancient astronaut theory. Sitchin provided a more detailed textual analysis (using Sumerian cuneiform). They are sometimes grouped together but had different methodologies.
