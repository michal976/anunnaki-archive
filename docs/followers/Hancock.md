# Graham Hancock

**Graham Hancock** (born 1950) is a British author known for his theories about a lost advanced civilization destroyed by a cataclysm at the end of the last Ice Age.

## Key Works

- *Fingerprints of the Gods* (1995)
- *Heaven's Mirror* (1998)
- *Underworld* (2002)
- *Magicians of the Gods* (2015)

## Relationship to Sitchin

Hancock cited Sitchin's work on the precessional cycle and the dating of the Giza monuments. He disagrees with Sitchin on some points but shares the view that ancient civilizations possessed advanced knowledge.
