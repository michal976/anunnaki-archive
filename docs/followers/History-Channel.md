# History Channel

The History Channel's program *Ancient Aliens* (2009–present) has been the primary vehicle for popularizing the ancient astronaut hypothesis and Sitchin's work.

## The Show

- Premiered in 2009
- Features Sitchin's work extensively
- Has aired for over 20 seasons
- Presented by Giorgio Tsoukalos

## Impact

The show brought Sitchin's ideas to a mainstream audience but also faced criticism for:
- Lack of academic rigor
- Sensationalism
- Presenting speculation as fact
