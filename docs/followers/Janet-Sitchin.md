# Janet Sitchin

**Janet Sitchin** was the wife of Zecharia Sitchin and managed his literary estate after his death in 2010.

## Role

- Managed Sitchin's copyrights and publications
- Maintained his website and public presence
- Published posthumous collections of his work
- Promoted his legacy
