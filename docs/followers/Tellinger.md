# Michael Tellinger

**Michael Tellinger** (born 1950) is a South African author and researcher who studied the ancient stone circles of southern Africa.

## Key Claims

- Southern Africa contains the remains of an ancient Anunnaki settlement
- The Adam's Calendar (South Africa) is the oldest man-made structure on Earth
- The stone circles were part of a gold mining operation
- The Anunnaki used sound technology for construction

## Relationship to Sitchin

Tellinger extends Sitchin's claims about the African gold mines, providing physical evidence from the African continent.
