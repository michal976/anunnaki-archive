# The Anunnaki — An Obsidian Vault Based on Zecharia Sitchin's Earth Chronicles

Welcome to a comprehensive digital library exploring the Anunnaki narrative as reconstructed by Zecharia Sitchin across his seven-volume [[Earth Chronicles]] series. This vault compiles and cross-references the key themes, locations, characters, concepts, timelines, and scholarly critiques of one of the most provocative alternative historical theories of the twentieth century.

## What Are the Earth Chronicles?

Zecharia Sitchin (1920–2010), a scholar of ancient Near Eastern languages and the Bible, proposed that [[Nibiru]] — a hypothetical planet in our solar system with a 3,600-year orbital period — is the homeworld of the [[Anunnaki]], an extraterrestrial civilization that visited Earth approximately 450,000 years ago. According to Sitchin's reading of Sumerian cuneiform tablets, the Anunnaki came to Earth seeking gold, which they needed to repair their planet's atmosphere. They established colonies in [[E.DIN]] (the original "Garden of Eden" in Mesopotamia), genetically engineered [[Adamu|Homo sapiens]] as a worker species, and profoundly shaped the early civilizations of Sumer, [[Babylon]], Egypt, and the Indus Valley.
