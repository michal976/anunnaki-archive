# Archeoastronomie Gízy (Archeoastronomy of Giza)

The [[Gíza|Giza]] pyramid complex shows remarkable astronomical alignments.

## Key Alignments

1. **Orion Correlation** — The three pyramids align with Orion's Belt
2. **True North** — The Great Pyramid is aligned to true north within 0.05 degrees
3. **Solar Alignments** — The pyramids align with solstices and equinoxes
4. **Shafts** — The Great Pyramid's shafts point to Orion (Osiris) and Sirius (Isis)

## The Orion Correlation Theory

Proposed by [[Robert Bauval]], this theory argues that the Giza pyramids were built to mirror the stars of Orion's Belt, dating the original design to ~10,500 BCE.
