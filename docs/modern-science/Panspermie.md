# Panspermie (Panspermia)

Panspermia is the hypothesis that life exists throughout the universe and was distributed by meteoroids, asteroids, comets, or spacecraft.

## Types

- **Natural panspermia** — Life travels naturally through space
- **Directed panspermia** — Life is intentionally seeded by an advanced civilization
- **Lithopanspermia** — Life travels inside rocks

## Sitchin's Connection

Sitchin's narrative can be seen as a form of "directed panspermia" — the [[Anunnaki]] intentionally seeded life on Earth through genetic engineering.
