# Theory of Ancient Astronauts (Ancient Astronaut Theory)

The ancient astronaut hypothesis proposes that extraterrestrial beings visited Earth in antiquity and influenced human civilization.

## Key Proponents

- [[Erich von Däniken]] — *Chariots of the Gods* (1968)
- [[Zecharia Sitchin]] — *The 12th Planet* (1976)
- [[Graham Hancock]] — *Fingerprints of the Gods* (1995)
- [[Robert Bauval]] — *The Orion Mystery* (1994)

## Key Claims

- Ancient texts describe extraterrestrial visitors
- Megalithic structures required advanced technology
- Human evolution was influenced by external intervention
- Religious traditions preserve memories of contact
