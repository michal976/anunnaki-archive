# African Mines

Southern Africa contains archaeological evidence of extensive ancient mining operations.

## The Evidence

- Gold mines in South Africa, Zimbabwe, and Botswana
- Mining tunnels dating back 80,000+ years
- The "Great Zimbabwe" stone complex
- Evidence of advanced geological knowledge
