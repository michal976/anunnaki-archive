# Sinaj (Sinai)

The **Sinai Peninsula** is a triangular land bridge connecting [[Africa]] and [[Asia]], bordered by the [[Mediterranean Sea]] to the north and the [[Red Sea]] to the south. In the Bible, it is the location of [[Mount Sinai]] where [[Moses]] received the Ten Commandments. For Zecharia Sitchin, the Sinai Peninsula was the **primary spaceport of the Anunnaki** on Earth.

## Sitchin's Central Thesis

Sitchin identified the central Sinai plateau — specifically the area around the mountain known to the Egyptians as the "Stairway of the Gods" — as the main landing and take-off facility for the [[Anunnaki]].

> *"The Sinai Peninsula, with its flat, elevated central plateau, was the ideal location for a spaceport. The approach path was marked by a series of landmarks culminating in the Giza pyramid complex — the ultimate navigational beacon."*

## The "Stairway of the Gods"

The Egyptians called certain mountains in the Sinai region *Ta-ur* ("the stairway"), and Sitchin connected this to the biblical "ladder" of [[Jacob]]'s dream and the Sumerian concept of the [[DUR.AN.KI]] — the "Bond Heaven-Earth."

## Supporting Evidence

Sitchin cited several pieces of evidence:

1. **Geographical suitability** — The central Sinai plateau is flat, elevated (1,500–2,000 meters), and remote
2. **Sacred mountains** — [[Mount Sinai]] (Jebel Musa), [[Mount Horeb]], and Mount Serbal were considered holy in multiple traditions
3. **Monastic traditions** — St. Catherine's Monastery, built at the purported site of the Burning Bush, preserves the memory of a sacred place
4. **Biblical narrative** — The *Exodus* account describes divine presence on the mountain with smoke, fire, and thunder — which Sitchin read as a spacecraft landing
5. **The "Landing Corridor"** — The alignment of [[Ararat]], [[Jeruzalém]], [[Baalbek]], and [[Gíza]] all point to Sinai

## The Exodus Connection

Sitchin reinterpreted the biblical story of Moses and the Exodus as a conflict over control of the spaceport:

- **Moses** was an educated Egyptian prince who knew of the Anunnaki presence
- **The ten plagues** were environmental disasters engineered by the Anunnaki
- **The parting of the Red Sea** was a manipulation of tidal forces
- **The destruction of the golden calf** was the destruction of a rival deity's symbol
- **Moses' "face shining"** after descending from Mount Sinai was radiation exposure from the spacecraft
