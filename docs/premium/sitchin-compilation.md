# 📚 Earth Chronicles — Full Compilation

> **Premium content** — exclusive to members.

This compilation is curated from all seven books of Zecharia Sitchin's *Earth Chronicles* series, organizing the key evidence into a single reference document.

## Book 1: The 12th Planet (1976)

### The Solar System According to Sumer

The Sumerians depicted a solar system with twelve celestial bodies — including a dark planet on a vast elliptical orbit, Nibiru, entering between Mars and Jupiter every 3,600 years. The Enuma Elish describes this planet's collision with Tiamat, shattering it into the asteroid belt and Earth.

**Key evidence:** Sumerian cylinder seal VA/243 (Berlin Museum) shows the Sun surrounded by eleven planets — including a large one between Mars and Jupiter. No telescope existed. They knew.

### The Genetic Creation

Enki proposed binding "the image of the gods" onto existing hominids. Ninmah (Ninharsag) performed the genetic engineering. The Atra-Hasis epic describes mixing "clay" (hominid) with the "flesh and blood" of a god (Anunnaki DNA). The result: *Homo sapiens* — appearing suddenly 300,000 years ago.

### The Gold Mines

The AB.ZU (southeastern Africa) held vast gold deposits. The Anunnaki mined for 100,000+ years before creating humans. Modern mining engineers have dated these tunnels to 50,000–100,000 years old.

## Book 2: The Stairway to Heaven (1980)

### The Landing Corridor

Aprecisely aligned chain of sites: Ararat → Baalbek → Giza → Sinai. The Giza pyramids were guidance beacons. Baalbek was the actual Landing Place — with 1,200-ton stone blocks no modern crane can move.

### Tilmun — The Spaceport

After the Deluge, the Anunnaki established a new spaceport in the Sinai — Tilmun ("Land of the Rocketships"). It was controlled from a Mission Control Center on Mount Moriah (Jerusalem).

## Book 3: The Wars of Gods and Men (1985)

### The Pyramid Wars (8670 B.C.)

Ninurta stormed the Great Pyramid, stripping it of its equipment — leaving it empty, as it was found. The "radiant place" inside was a power installation.

### The Nuclear Holocaust (2024 B.C.)

Erra (Nergal) unleashed "seven awesome weapons" on the Sinai spaceport. Sodom and Gomorrah were collateral damage. The radioactive "Evil Wind" swept east, killing Sumer. Libyan Desert Glass is the evidence — trinitite from thermonuclear airburst.

## Book 4: The Lost Realms (1990)

### The Anunnaki in the Americas

After the nuclear disaster, Thoth (Ningishzidda) relocated Anunnaki operations to the Americas. The Olmec colossal heads — African faces carved in Mexican stone. Tiahuanaco — "Baalbek of the New World" at 12,500 feet. The Uru people speak a language with Sumerian roots.

### The Calendars

The Mayan Long Count begins August 13, 3113 B.C. — a date the Anunnaki arrived in the Americas. The 260-day *Tzolkin* encodes Nibiru orbital mechanics.

## Book 5: When Time Began (1993)

### The Precessional Calendar

The Sumerian calendar of 360 days encodes Nibiru's orbit. The zodiac divides into 12 houses of 30°, each corresponding to 2,160 years of precession. The divine calendar: 1 SAR (3,600 years) = 1 Nibiru year.

### Stonehenge

A computer for tracking the precessional cycle — built by Anunnaki knowledge, not primitive Britons.

## Book 6: The Cosmic Code (1998)

### The DNA Code

The Hebrew alphabet encodes a "cosmic code" linking human DNA to celestial cycles. The number 22 (letters in Hebrew alphabet) matches chromosome pair structure.

### The Celestial Counterpart

Every event on Earth has a celestial counterpart — the principle of "as above, so below." The precessional ages mark turning points in Anunnaki rule: Taurus (Enlil's age), Aries (Marduk's takeover), Pisces (human age), Aquarius (return of the gods).

## Book 7: The End of Days (2007)

### The Cycle of Return

The prophesied "End of Days" is not the end of the world — it is the end of a celestial cycle. Nibiru's return coincides with upheavals every 3,600 years. The last passage: ~1650 B.C. (Exodus era). Next passage: foretold.

### The Modern Parallel

21st-century conflicts mirror those of 4,000 years ago. The "kings of the East" and "Gog and Magog" are not supernatural — they are the return of the Anunnaki factions.
