# Letadla a rakety (Aircraft and Rockets)

Selected quotations describing flying vehicles and spacecraft in ancient texts, as interpreted by Zecharia Sitchin.

## The SHEM (Rocketship)

> *"Let us build us a city and a tower, whose top may reach unto heaven; and let us make us a SHEM — a rocketship — that we may ascend to the heavens."* — Sitchin's reading of Genesis 11:4

> *"The MU, the shining vehicle, ascended to heaven like a great bird. The people watched in awe as it rose to the abode of Anu."* — Sumerian text

## Ezekiel's Vision

> *"And I looked, and behold, a whirlwind came out of the north, a great cloud, and a fire infolding itself... And out of the midst thereof came the likeness of four living creatures."* — Ezekiel 1:4-5

> *"And when the living creatures went, the wheels went by them... for the spirit of the living creature was in the wheels."* — Ezekiel 1:19-20

## The "Whirlwind" that Took Elijah

> *"And it came to pass, as they still went on, and talked, that, behold, there appeared a chariot of fire, and horses of fire, and parted them both asunder; and Elijah went up by a whirlwind into heaven."* — 2 Kings 2:11

## The Chariot of the Gods

> *"His chariot was like a storm, his steeds were like the wind, and he rode upon the clouds."* — Description of the storm god
