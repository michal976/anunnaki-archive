# Creation of Man

Selected quotations describing the creation of humanity in Sumerian, biblical, and other texts.

## The Atra-Hasis Account

> *"Let the mother goddess mix clay with the flesh and blood of a god. Then they will become a god and man together, living in harmony forever."*

> *"Nintu, the lady of birth, mixed the clay. The gods spat upon the clay. She summoned the great Anunnaki to witness the creation."*

> *"Seven males and seven females were created. They were the first humans — the Lullu, the primitive workers."*

## The Biblical Account

> *"And the LORD God formed man of the dust of the ground, and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life; and man became a living soul."* (Genesis 2:7)

> *"Let us make man in our image, after our likeness..."* (Genesis 1:26)

## The "Debate between Enki and Ninmah"

> *"Enki created a being — but it was imperfect. Ninmah created beings — but they were imperfect. They argued about their creations, and the debate revealed the difficulty of the work."*

## Sitchin's Summary

> *"The creation of man was not a miracle — it was a scientific procedure. The Anunnaki took the existing hominid of Earth — Homo erectus — and added their own genetic material. The result was Homo sapiens — a perfect hybrid."*
