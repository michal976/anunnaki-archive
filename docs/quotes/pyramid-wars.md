# Pyramid Wars

Selected quotations from texts that Zecharia Sitchin interpreted as describing the conflicts among the Anunnaki — the [Pyramid Wars](../timeline/pyramid-wars.md).

## The Conflict between Marduk and Enlil

> *"Marduk, the firstborn of Enki, said in his heart: 'I will raise my city. I will make it the greatest among the cities of the gods. I will be supreme.'"*

> *"Enlil heard the words of Marduk and was angry. 'He seeks to take my place,' he said to the assembly of the gods. 'He seeks to sit upon my throne.'"*

## The Destruction of the Spaceport

> *"The weapon of the great ones was unleashed. The house of the SHEM was destroyed. The bond of heaven and earth was broken."*

> *"On that day, the spaceport was no more. The gods could no longer come and go between heaven and earth. The link with Nibiru was severed."*

## The "Confusion of Tongues"

> *"The Lord said: 'Behold, they are one people, and they have one language. They have begun to build a tower. Now nothing they propose to do will be impossible for them. Let us go down and confuse their language.'" — Genesis 11:6-7

## The Aftermath

> *"After the great battle, Marduk fled to the west. The other gods scattered across the earth. The golden age of the gods was over."*

> *"The great lord Enlil departed. The command center at Nippur fell silent. The gods no longer walked among men."*
