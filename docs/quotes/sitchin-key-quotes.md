# Sitchinovy klíčové výroky (Sitchin's Key Statements)

Selected quotations directly from Zecharia Sitchin's writings that summarize his key arguments.

## The Central Thesis

> *"All the ancient civilizations of the Near East — the Sumerians, the Babylonians, the Assyrians, the Egyptians, the Hebrews — had a common source. That source was the knowledge brought to Earth by the Anunnaki from the planet Nibiru."*

> *"The 12th Planet is not a work of fiction. It is a reconstruction of ancient history based on a careful reading of the Sumerian texts."*

## On the Anunnaki

> *"The gods of antiquity were not figments of primitive imagination. They were real beings — flesh and blood — who came from another world."*

> *"The Anunnaki were not spiritual beings. They were astronauts from the planet Nibiru who colonized Earth approximately 450,000 years ago."*

## On the Enuma Elish

> *"The Enuma Elish is not a myth. It is a scientifically accurate account of the creation of our solar system, encoded in the language of myth."*

## On Genesis

> *"The biblical Book of Genesis is not a revelation from God. It is a carefully edited version of much older Sumerian accounts."*

## On Humanity

> *"We are not the product of random evolution. We are a genetically engineered species — created by the Anunnaki to serve as workers in their gold mines."*

## On the Purpose

> *"The Anunnaki came to Earth for gold. They needed it to protect their planet's atmosphere. The gold was ground into a fine powder and suspended in the upper atmosphere of Nibiru."*

## On the Future

> *"Nibiru will return. The cycle of 3,600 years will bring it back into the inner solar system. And when it does, the destiny of humanity will be determined."*
