# Číselné kódy (Numerical Codes)

Selected quotations and examples of numerical patterns that Zecharia Sitchin identified in Sumerian, Babylonian, and biblical texts.

## The Number 3,600 (NÉRU)

> *"The NÉRU is 3,600 years — the orbit of the great planet Nibiru. The gods of heaven counted time in NÉRU, not in years."*

> *"Alulim of Eridu reigned 28,800 years = 8 NÉRU. Alalgar of Eridu reigned 36,000 years = 10 NÉRU."* — Sumerian King List

## The Number 50

> *"The fifty great gods of the Anunnaki who decree the fates, the fifty who sit in the council of Enlil..."*

> *"The sacred number of Enlil is 50 — the highest after Anu's 60."*

## The Number 12

> *"The twelve great gods of heaven, the twelve months of the year, the twelve houses of the zodiac..."*

> *"Twelve is the number of the celestial council, and of the tribes of Israel, and of the apostles of the son."*

## The Precessional Numbers

> *"72 is the number of degrees in one zodiac age — the number of years it takes for the stars to shift one degree."*

> *"2,160 years is one zodiac age — 1/12 of the 25,920-year precessional cycle."*

> *"The Great Year, the cycle of the ages, is 25,920 years — the time it takes for the equinox to complete its journey through all twelve houses."*
