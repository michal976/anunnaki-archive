# Egypt — Egyptian Religion

Zecharia Sitchin argued that Egyptian religion was heavily influenced by the [[Anunnaki]], particularly after [[Marduk]] fled to Egypt following the Pyramid Wars.

## Key Figures in Sitchin's Reading

| Egyptian God | Anunnaki Identity |
|-------------|-------------------|
| [[Ra]] / [[Amun]] | [[Marduk]] |
| [[Thoth]] | An Anunnaki scribe/sage |
| [[Osiris]] | Possibly [[Enki]] or a son of Enki |
| [[Isis]] | [[Ninmah]] or a goddess |
| [[Horus]] | Son of Osiris and Isis |
| [[Set]] | [[Nergal]] or another warrior god |

## The Pyramids

Sitchin argued that the Egyptian pyramids were not tombs but Anunnaki navigation beacons.
