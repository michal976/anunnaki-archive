# Egypt — Egyptian Religion

Zecharia Sitchin argued that Egyptian religion was heavily influenced by the Anunnaki, particularly after [Marduk](../characters/Marduk.md) fled to Egypt following the Pyramid Wars.

## Key Figures in Sitchin's Reading

| Egyptian God | Anunnaki Identity |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Ra / Amun | [Marduk](../characters/Marduk.md) |
| Thoth | An Anunnaki scribe/sage |
| Osiris | Possibly [Enki](../characters/Enki.md) or a son of Enki |
| Isis | [Ninmah](../characters/Ninmah.md) or a goddess |
| Horus | Son of Osiris and Isis |
| Set | [Nergal](../characters/Nergal.md) or another warrior god |

## The Pyramids

Sitchin argued that the Egyptian pyramids were not tombs but Anunnaki navigation beacons.
