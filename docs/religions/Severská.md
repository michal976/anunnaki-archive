# Severská (Norse Religion)

While Sitchin focused primarily on Near Eastern and Mediterranean traditions, other researchers have extended his methodology to [[Severská|Norse mythology]].

## Norse-Anunnaki Parallels

| Norse God | Possible Anunnaki Counterpart |
|-----------|-------------------------------|
| [[Odin]] | [[Enlil]] — the wise ruler / all-father |
| [[Thor]] | [[Ishkur]] / [[Ninurta]] — storm/war god |
| [[Loki]] | [[Enki]] — the trickster |
| [[Freya]] | [[Inanna]] — goddess of love and war |
| [[Heimdall]] | [[Utu]] — the watchman / guardian |
| The '''Aesir''' | The [[Anunnaki]] |
| The '''Vanir''' | A rival group of gods |

## The World Tree (Yggdrasil)

The Norse concept of Yggdrasil, the world tree connecting the nine worlds, has been compared to the [[DUR.AN.KI]] — the bond connecting heaven and Earth.
