# Gnosticismus (Gnosticism)

Gnosticism was a religious movement in the early Christian era that emphasized secret knowledge (*gnosis*) as the path to salvation. Some parallels between Gnostic teachings and Sitchin's Anunnaki narrative are noteworthy.

## Gnostic Parallels

| Gnostic Concept | Anunnaki Parallel |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| The Demiurge (lesser creator god) | [Enlil](../characters/Enlil.md) — the stern commander |
| The Archons (rulers of the material world) | The Anunnaki |
| Secret knowledge saved from the creator | The [ME](../concepts/ME.md) — hidden wisdom |
| The "alien God" beyond the Demiurge | [Anu](../characters/Anu.md) — the distant king of Nibiru |
| The serpent as a giver of knowledge | [Enki](../characters/Enki.md) — the wise serpent god |

## Sitchin's View

Sitchin noted that Gnostics preserved the idea that the creator of the material world (the Demiurge) was not the supreme God — a concept that aligns with his reading of [Enlil](../characters/Enlil.md) as a lesser (though powerful) being compared to [Anu](../characters/Anu.md).
