# Nový zákon (New Testament)

Some researchers, building on Zecharia Sitchin's work, have explored potential connections between the Anunnaki narrative and the [New Testament](new-testament.md).

## Possible Connections

- **Jesus as an Anunnaki figure** — Some theorists suggest Jesus may have been an Anunnaki emissary
- **The Second Coming** — The return of Nibiru interpreted as the return of a god
- **The Book of Revelation** — Descriptions of cosmic events possibly related to Nibiru
- **The "Son of Man"** — A figure coming on clouds with power

## Sitchin's View

Sitchin was cautious about directly connecting Jesus to the Anunnaki, focusing primarily on the Old Testament. However, he noted that the apocalyptic literature of the New Testament (Revelation) contains imagery consistent with his interpretation of celestial events.
