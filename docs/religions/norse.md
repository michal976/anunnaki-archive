# Norse Religion

While Sitchin focused primarily on Near Eastern and Mediterranean traditions, other researchers have extended his methodology to [Norse mythology](norse.md).

## Norse-Anunnaki Parallels

| Norse God | Possible Anunnaki Counterpart |
|-----------|-------------------------------|
| Odin | [Enlil](../characters/Enlil.md) — the wise ruler / all-father |
| Thor | [Ishkur](../characters/Ishkur.md) / [Ninurta](../characters/Ninurta.md) — storm/war god |
| Loki | [Enki](../characters/Enki.md) — the trickster |
| Freya | [Inanna](../characters/Inanna.md) — goddess of love and war |
| Heimdall | [Utu](../characters/Utu.md) — the watchman / guardian |
| The '''Aesir''' | The Anunnaki |
| The '''Vanir''' | A rival group of gods |

## The World Tree (Yggdrasil)

The Norse concept of Yggdrasil, the world tree connecting the nine worlds, has been compared to the [DUR.AN.KI](../concepts/DUR.AN.KI.md) — the bond connecting heaven and Earth.
