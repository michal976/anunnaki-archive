# Řecko (Greek Religion)

The mythology of ancient [[Řecko|Greece]] shows clear parallels with the Mesopotamian and Anunnaki narratives, according to Zecharia Sitchin.

## Parallels

| Greek God | Anunnaki Counterpart |
|-----------|----------------------|
| [[Zeus]] | [[Enlil]] — sky god, ruler |
| [[Poseidon]] | [[Enki]] — god of the waters |
| [[Hades]] | [[Nergal]] — god of the underworld |
| [[Aphrodite]] | [[Inanna]] — goddess of love |
| [[Ares]] | [[Ninurta]] — god of war |
| [[Hermes]] | [[Nabu]] — messenger god |
| [[Prometheus]] | [[Enki]] — creator and benefactor of humans |
| [[Atlas]] | The Anunnaki who held up the sky |

## The Titans

The Greek myth of the [[Titans]] — the older generation of gods overthrown by the Olympians — parallels Sitchin's narrative of the older Anunnaki generation ([[Anu]], [[Enlil]], [[Enki]]) being challenged by the younger generation ([[Marduk]], [[Inanna]]).
