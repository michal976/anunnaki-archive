# Sources

This folder catalogs the primary and secondary sources that form the textual foundation of Zecharia Sitchin's Earth Chronicles. Sitchin based his theories on his own translations of Sumerian, Akkadian, Babylonian, and Assyrian cuneiform tablets, as well as his interpretations of biblical texts.

## Primary Mesopotamian Sources

| Source | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| [[Enuma-Elíš]] | The Babylonian creation epic describing Marduk's rise and the celestial battle |
| [[Atra-Hasis]] | The Akkadian flood epic detailing the creation of humans and the Great Flood |
| [[Bible-Genesis]] | The biblical account of creation, the Garden of Eden, and the flood |
