# Enlilovo velení (Enlil's Command / The Future)

The "Enlil Command" refers to the future return of the [[Anunnaki]] as prophesied in various ancient texts. According to Zecharia Sitchin's reading, [[Enlil]] decreed that the Anunnaki would depart but eventually return when [[Nibiru]] completes its next orbit.

## The Prophecy

- The Anunnaki will return when Nibiru approaches Earth
- The cycle of 3,600 years will bring them back
- The "Day of the Lord" in biblical prophecy refers to this event
- The return will bring both destruction and renewal

## Sitchin's Prediction

Sitchin suggested that the next crossing of Nibiru would occur in the near future (possibly around 2900 CE, based on his calculations).
