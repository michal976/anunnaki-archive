# Nukleární katastrofa (Nuclear Catastrophe)

**Date: 2024 BCE**

The nuclear destruction of [[Sippar]] and [[Ur]] was the final act of the [[Pyramidové-války|Pyramid Wars]]. A nuclear weapon was used to destroy the spaceport and the rebellious cities.

## The Event

- Authorized by [[Enlil]] and [[Ninurta]]
- Target: [[Sippar]] (spaceport) and [[Ur]]
- A nuclear bomb or directed-energy weapon
- The "evil wind" described in Sumerian texts

## The Consequences

- The spaceport was destroyed, severing contact with [[Nibiru]]
- Sumerian civilization collapsed
- The Anunnaki began their withdrawal from Earth
- [[Marduk]] fled to Egypt and the Americas
- The "gods" stopped walking among men
