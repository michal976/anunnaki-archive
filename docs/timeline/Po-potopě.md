# Po potopě (After the Flood)

**Date: Approximately 13,000–3,800 BCE**

After the Great Flood, the [[Anunnaki]] re-established their presence on Earth. This period saw the formation of the first post-diluvian cities and the institution of kingship.

## The Second Settlement

The Anunnaki re-established their cities:
- [[Eridu]] — Rebuilt by Enki
- [[Nippur]] — Enlil's command center
- [[Sippar]] — The spaceport
- [[Ur]] — Nannar's center

## The Institution of Kingship

The Sumerian King List records that "kingship descended from heaven again" after the flood, first at [[Kiš]]. The early post-diluvian kings had extraordinarily long reigns — which Sitchin interpreted as NÉRU-based measurements.

## The Rise of Civilization

This period saw the gradual emergence of human civilization under Anunnaki guidance:
- Agriculture and irrigation
- Writing
- Law and government
- Astronomy and mathematics
- Metallurgy and crafts
