# Potopa (The Great Flood)

**Date: Approximately 13,000 BCE**

Sitchin dated the Great Flood to about 13,000 BCE. It was caused by the gravitational effects of [[Nibiru]]'s approach, which triggered massive tidal waves and the melting of ice caps.

## The Cause

The flood was not a random natural disaster — it was a cyclical event tied to Nibiru's orbit. [[Enlil]] used the approaching flood as an opportunity to destroy humanity, but [[Enki]] saved them by warning [[Ziusudra]] (Noah).

## The Survivors

- [[Ziusudra]] / [[Utnapishtim]] — The flood hero
- A boat was built containing humans and animals
- After the flood, the boat landed on [[Ararat]]

## The Aftermath

The Anunnaki regrouped after the flood and re-established civilization. The "kingship" was lowered from heaven again at [[Kiš]].
