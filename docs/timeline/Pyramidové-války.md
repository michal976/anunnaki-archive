# Pyramidové války (Pyramid Wars)

**Date: Approximately 3,500–2,024 BCE**

The [[Pyramidové-války|Pyramid Wars]] were the conflicts among the [[Anunnaki]] over control of Earth and its spaceport facilities.

## The Major Events

1. [[Marduk]] builds up [[Babylon]] and seeks supremacy
2. Marduk builds the "Tower of Babel" — a rocket launch tower (the [[SHEM]])
3. [[Enlil]] confronts Marduk
4. The "confusion of tongues" — destruction of the tower
5. Escalation of hostilities
6. The nuclear strike of 2024 BCE

## The Result

- The spaceport at [[Sippar]] is destroyed
- Marduk flees to exile
- Enlil's authority is damaged
- The Anunnaki begin their withdrawal from Earth
