# Přistání v Eridu (Landing at Eridu)

**Date: Approximately 450,000 BCE**

According to Zecharia Sitchin, the first landing of the [[Anunnaki]] on Earth occurred at [[Eridu]], in the region later known as Sumer (southern Mesopotamia).

## The Significance

Eridu was chosen as the first landing site because:
- It was in the fertile plain of the [[E.DIN]]
- It provided access to water from the [[Euphrates]] and the Persian Gulf
- It was geographically suited for a base of operations

## The First Settlement

The Anunnaki built their first Earth base at Eridu, which included:
- A landing field or spaceport
- Living quarters
- A control center
- The [[Apsu]] — a sacred water source
