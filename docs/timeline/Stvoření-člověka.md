# Stvoření člověka (Creation of Humanity)

**Date: Approximately 445,000 BCE**

After the [[Igigi]] rebellion, [[Enki]] proposed creating a "primitive worker" — a hybrid being that could perform the labor of the mines. This led to the genetic engineering of [[Adamu]], the first [[Homo sapiens]].

## The Procedure

According to the [[Atra-Hasis]] epic and Sitchin's reading:
1. The genetic material of an Anunnaki male was combined with the egg of a female hominid ([[Homo erectus]])
2. The resulting embryo was gestated in a "womb of birth" (laboratory)
3. [[Ninmah]] (Nintur) performed the genetic manipulation
4. Seven pairs of humans were created — the first generation

## The Result

The result was *Homo sapiens* — a hybrid species with:
- The physical form of the Anunnaki
- Human cognitive abilities enhanced with Anunnaki traits
- The ability to reproduce
- A shorter lifespan than the Anunnaki
