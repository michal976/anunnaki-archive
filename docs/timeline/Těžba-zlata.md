# Těžba zlata (Gold Mining)

**Date: Approximately 450,000–445,000 BCE**

After establishing their base at [[Eridu]], the [[Anunnaki]] began mining gold. This was the primary purpose of their mission to Earth.

## The Gold Mines

The Anunnaki established gold mines in southeast [[Africa]], in the region of modern-day [[South Africa]], [[Zimbabwe]], and [[Botswana]]. These are now known as the "Ancient Gold Mines" of the [[Africké-doly|African]] continent.

## The Miners

The actual mining labor was performed by the [[Igigi]], a group of 300 lower-ranking Anunnaki. They worked for one Nibiru year (3,600 Earth years) before rebelling.

## The Rebellion

The Igigi's rebellion (recorded in the [[Atra-Hasis]] epic) led directly to the creation of [[Adamu|humanity]] as a replacement workforce.
