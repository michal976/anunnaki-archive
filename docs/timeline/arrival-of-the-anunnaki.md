# Arrival of the Anunnaki

**Date: Approximately 450,000 BCE**

According to Zecharia Sitchin, the Anunnaki arrived on Earth approximately 450,000 years ago. They came from [Nibiru](../characters/Nibiru.md), the twelfth planet, in a fleet of spacecraft seeking gold to repair their planet's atmosphere.

## The Landing

The first landing site was [Eridu](../concepts/Eridu.md), in what is now southern Iraq. The Anunnaki established a base camp and began surveying the planet for resources.

## The Leadership

The initial expedition was led by:
- [Enki](../characters/Enki.md) — Chief scientist and expedition leader
- [Enlil](../characters/Enlil.md) — Commander of the mission
- [Anu](../characters/Anu.md) — King of Nibiru (who remained in orbit or visited occasionally)

## The Purpose

The Anunnaki came to Earth for gold, which they needed to create a reflective shield to protect Nibiru's atmosphere from solar radiation.
