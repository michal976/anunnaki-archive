# Creation of Humanity

> **Sumerian term:** LULLU (𒇽𒇽) — "Primitive Worker"
> **Sumerian term:** ADAMU (𒀀𒁕𒈬) — "Earth creature"

**Date: Approximately 445,000 BCE**

After the [Igigi](../concepts/Igigi.md) rebellion, [Enki](../characters/Enki.md) proposed creating a "primitive worker" — a hybrid being that could perform the labor of the mines. This led to the genetic engineering of [Adamu](../concepts/Adamu.md), the first Homo sapiens.

## The Procedure

According to the [Atra Hasis](../sources/Atra-Hasis.md) epic and Sitchin's reading:
1. The genetic material of an Anunnaki male was combined with the egg of a female hominid (Homo erectus)
2. The resulting embryo was gestated in a "womb of birth" (laboratory)
3. [Ninmah](../characters/Ninmah.md) (Nintur) performed the genetic manipulation
4. Seven pairs of humans were created — the first generation

## The Result

The result was *Homo sapiens* — a hybrid species with:
- The physical form of the Anunnaki
- Human cognitive abilities enhanced with Anunnaki traits
- The ability to reproduce
- A shorter lifespan than the Anunnaki
