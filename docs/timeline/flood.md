# The Great Flood

> **Sumerian term:** A.MA.RU (𒀭𒈤𒊒) — "the Deluge / Flood"

**Date: Approximately 13,000 BCE**

Sitchin dated the Great Flood to about 13,000 BCE. It was caused by the gravitational effects of [Nibiru](../characters/Nibiru.md)'s approach, which triggered massive tidal waves and the melting of ice caps.

## The Cause

The flood was not a random natural disaster — it was a cyclical event tied to Nibiru's orbit. [Enlil](../characters/Enlil.md) used the approaching flood as an opportunity to destroy humanity, but [Enki](../characters/Enki.md) saved them by warning Ziusudra (Noah).

## The Survivors

- Ziusudra / Utnapishtim — The flood hero
- A boat was built containing humans and animals
- After the flood, the boat landed on [Ararat](../places/Ararat.md)

## The Aftermath

The Anunnaki regrouped after the flood and re-established civilization. The "kingship" was lowered from heaven again at Kiš.
