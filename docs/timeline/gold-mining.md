# Gold Mining

> **Sumerian term:** KUG.GI (𒆬𒄀) — "Gold"
> **Sumerian term:** AŠ.NA (𒀸𒈾) — "the ore"

**Date: Approximately 450,000–445,000 BCE**

After establishing their base at [Eridu](../concepts/Eridu.md), the Anunnaki began mining gold. This was the primary purpose of their mission to Earth.

## The Gold Mines

The Anunnaki established gold mines in southeast Africa, in the region of modern-day South Africa, Zimbabwe, and Botswana. These are now known as the "Ancient Gold Mines" of the [African](../modern-science/african-mines.md) continent.

## The Miners

The actual mining labor was performed by the [Igigi](../concepts/Igigi.md), a group of 300 lower-ranking Anunnaki. They worked for one Nibiru year (3,600 Earth years) before rebelling.

## The Rebellion

The Igigi's rebellion (recorded in the [Atra Hasis](../sources/Atra-Hasis.md) epic) led directly to the creation of [humanity](../concepts/Adamu.md) as a replacement workforce.
