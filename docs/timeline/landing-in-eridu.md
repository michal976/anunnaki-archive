# Landing at Eridu

**Date: Approximately 450,000 BCE**

According to Zecharia Sitchin, the first landing of the Anunnaki on Earth occurred at [Eridu](../concepts/Eridu.md), in the region later known as Sumer (southern Mesopotamia).

## The Significance

Eridu was chosen as the first landing site because:
- It was in the fertile plain of the [E.Din](../concepts/E.DIN.md)
- It provided access to water from the Euphrates and the Persian Gulf
- It was geographically suited for a base of operations

## The First Settlement

The Anunnaki built their first Earth base at Eridu, which included:
- A landing field or spaceport
- Living quarters
- A control center
- The [Apsu](../places/Apsu.md) — a sacred water source
