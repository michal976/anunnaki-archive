# Nuclear Catastrophe

> **Sumerian term:** NAM.ḪUL (𒉆𒃲) — "Weapons of Terror"
> **Sumerian term:** GIG (𒄀𒅅) — "Catastrophe"

**Date: 2024 BCE**

The nuclear destruction of [Sippar](../places/Sippar.md) and [Ur](../places/Ur.md) was the final act of the [Pyramid Wars](pyramid-wars.md). A nuclear weapon was used to destroy the spaceport and the rebellious cities.

## The Event

- Authorized by [Enlil](../characters/Enlil.md) and [Ninurta](../characters/Ninurta.md)
- Target: [Sippar](../places/Sippar.md) (spaceport) and [Ur](../places/Ur.md)
- A nuclear bomb or directed-energy weapon
- The "evil wind" described in Sumerian texts

## The Consequences

- The spaceport was destroyed, severing contact with [Nibiru](../characters/Nibiru.md)
- Sumerian civilization collapsed
- The Anunnaki began their withdrawal from Earth
- [Marduk](../characters/Marduk.md) fled to Egypt and the Americas
- The "gods" stopped walking among men
