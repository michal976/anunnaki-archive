# Pyramid Wars

> **Sumerian term:** É.KUR (𒂍𒆳) — "Mountain House"

**Date: Approximately 3,500–2,024 BCE**

The [Pyramid Wars](pyramid-wars.md) were the conflicts among the Anunnaki over control of Earth and its spaceport facilities.

## The Major Events

1. [Marduk](../characters/Marduk.md) builds up [Babylon](../places/Babylon.md) and seeks supremacy
2. Marduk builds the "Tower of Babel" — a rocket launch tower (the [SHEM](../concepts/SHEM.md))
3. [Enlil](../characters/Enlil.md) confronts Marduk
4. The "confusion of tongues" — destruction of the tower
5. Escalation of hostilities
6. The nuclear strike of 2024 BCE

## The Result

- The spaceport at [Sippar](../places/Sippar.md) is destroyed
- Marduk flees to exile
- Enlil's authority is damaged
- The Anunnaki begin their withdrawal from Earth
