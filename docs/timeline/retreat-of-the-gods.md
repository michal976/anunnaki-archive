# Departure of the Gods

**Date: After 2024 BCE**

After the nuclear destruction of Sumer, the Anunnaki gradually withdrew from direct involvement in human affairs. The "golden age" when gods walked among men came to an end.

## The Withdrawal

- The spaceport was destroyed — no more travel to [Nibiru](../characters/Nibiru.md)
- Enlil and his faction departed
- Marduk went into exile (Egypt, then the Americas)
- Other gods retreated to their remaining temples
- Humanity was left to govern itself

## The Legacy

The Anunnaki did not completely abandon humanity. They left behind:
- The knowledge of civilization (writing, law, agriculture)
- The sacred texts (which encode their science)
- The great monuments (pyramids, ziggurats)
- The promise of return

## The Future Return

Sitchin predicted that the Anunnaki would return when [Nibiru](../characters/Nibiru.md) completes its next orbit — at the time of the next "crossing."
